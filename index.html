Client Insights

ideaGarage is a journey of ideation. It begins with deep insight into clients and understanding their evolving needs. In partnership with Client Expansion, First Rain, and Buyer Network information will be surfaced through APIs that help the team determine the most pressing client challenges and goals. This prioritized view of the client's most pressing need becomes the basis for igniting new ideas and thinking.

Ideate

Grounded in a shared understanding of their client goals and needs, a team then explores new ideas and possibilities. It begins with a Spark - a disruptive charge that ignites. Teams are served up a series of industry-specific insights to unleash new thinking. Teams build expertise through Spark cards to help IBM solve their clients' most pressing business challenges.

  • Select Client Goals
  • Spark Cards
  • Select your Sparks
  • Sparks to Ideas

Prioritize

Create Ideas by customizing Sparks with specific client needs and insights relevant to your client. You can then submit Ideas individually to the Team Workplace, where they'll be considered and ranked in the Prioritize Phase.

  • Team Workspace
  • Evaluate
  • Promote

Build

A highly-rated Idea becomes an Opportunity Brief - a suggested plan of action. With your team, define key elements of the Brief by clearly articulating a compelling reason to act. These Briefs detail the required resources and capabilities, including how IBM products are a critical piece of your proposed solution.
